Commissioned Curiosities

Custom Orders

Some pieces are made quietly on their own. Others arrive because someone asks nicely.

If you have a vision, a need, or a beautifully unreasonable idea, this is where we begin.

Each custom piece is hand-wrought with care, conversation, and a proper respect for materials that occasionally have opinions of their own.

What Can Be Made

Custom work may include woodturned pieces, decorative objects, painted details, rustic curiosities, small seasonal works, and other hand-wrought notions.

If it lives somewhere between practical, beautiful, slightly enchanted, and difficult to explain — it may be exactly the right sort of thing.

How It Works

  1. You send your idea, inspiration, or wild little vision.
  2. We discuss size, materials, purpose, and any glorious specifics.
  3. Timing and pricing are agreed upon before work begins.
  4. Your piece is made slowly, properly, and with a touch of old-world stubbornness.

A Few Helpful Truths

Handmade means no two pieces are ever quite identical — which is part of the charm, part of the point, and occasionally part of the mischief.

Natural materials may vary in grain, color, texture, and temperament.

What Helps Most

  • Reference photos or inspiration images
  • Approximate size or intended use
  • Preferred colors, tones, or mood
  • Deadline (if you have one)
  • Your budget range, if helpful
