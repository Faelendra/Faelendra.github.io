Faelendra is the name the work answers to when it prefers not to be hurried.

She favors wood, earth, and wandering notions, and has been known to speak with materials until they agree to cooperate.

The name appeared first in the margins of ledgers, then on labels, and finally above the door—while the maker herself carried on quite sensibly, otherwise called Brenda.

Nothing here is mass-made or loudly explained.
Everything is wrought by hand, with care.

Established quietly. Enter kindly.

No curses included. Probably.
