About Faelendra

Works in Wood, Earth & Imagination

Faelendra is the name the work answers to when it prefers not to be hurried.

She favors wood, earth, and wandering notions, and has been known to speak with materials until they agree to cooperate.

The name first appeared in the margins of ledgers, then on labels, and finally above the door—while the maker herself carried on quite sensibly, otherwise called Brenda.

Wrought by Faelendra is a collection of handcrafted curiosities inspired by wood, earth, paint, and imagination. Nothing here is mass-made or loudly explained. Each piece is created with care, old-world craftsmanship, and a touch of quiet magic.

These are pieces gathered from stories, nature, and imagination— made to be treasured, gifted, and passed from one pair of hands to another.

Established quietly. Enter kindly.

No curses included, probably.
