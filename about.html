The Maker, The Name, The Notions

About Faelendra

Faelendra is the name the work answers to when it prefers not to be hurried.

She favors wood, earth, and wandering notions, and has been known to speak with materials until they agree to cooperate. The name first appeared in the margins of ledgers, then on labels, and finally above the door—while the maker herself carried on quite sensibly, otherwise called Brenda.

Wrought by Faelendra is a collection of hand-wrought curiosities and crafted works shaped in wood, earth, paint, and imagination. Nothing here is mass-made or loudly explained. Each piece is made with care, a touch of old-world reverence, and the occasional conversation with stubborn materials.

What you will find here are works with story in them—pieces meant to feel gathered rather than manufactured, discovered rather than merely bought. A little vintage, a little mystical, a little woodland, and always made by hand.

Established quietly. Enter kindly.
